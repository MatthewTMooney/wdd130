History

We started in 2025, by a one man with a dream, A Dream to get people having more outdoor fun. He spent weeks picking locations by testing them himself over the summer of 2024, and created a list.

He then got his equipment, his premises, and everything else he needed, and after a lot of hard work, Matthew finally opened Down by the River, becoming an instant classic with locals and tourists alike.
